## Patterns Observed

### Why Data Was Not Found (13 studies)
- **Qualitative reporting only:** 6 studies reported significance/direction but no numeric values
- **Wrong outcome reported:** 2 studies focused on different outcomes than requested
- **Truncated results text:** 2 studies had incomplete results sections in the provided text
- **Effect sizes only:** 1 study reported Cohen's d but not raw scores
- **Aggregated data:** 2 studies reported combined outcomes without specific breakdown

### Data Quality Issues
- Many studies report p-values and significance but omit actual mean/SD values
- Percentage-based reporting for binary outcomes requires back-calculation
- Continuous outcomes often lack SD/SE even when means are reported
- Results sections frequently truncated or incomplete in provided excerpts

## Recommendations
1. Request full results sections (tables preferred) rather than text excerpts
2. For continuous outcomes, explicitly request mean, SD, and n for each group
3. For binary outcomes, request raw event counts rather than percentages when possible
4. Verify that the outcome measure in the results text matches the requested outcome
